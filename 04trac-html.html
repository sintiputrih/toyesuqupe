04trac - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star and rapper who has gained popularity for his 04trac TikTok account where he posts comedy videos, often involving his pickup truck. His videos have earned him over 260,000 followers on the platform. 

Before Fame

He launched his TikTok account in January 2020. 

Trivia

One of his most popular TikTok videos is a comedy about him being a "country boy."

Family Life

He was born in Laurinburg, North Carolina. 

Associated With

He posted a TikTok video set to the DaBaby song "BLIND" in November 2020.
