Bryan Danielson And Jon Moxley To Team Up On 1/6 AEW Rampage, Updated Card

Bryan Danielson and Jon Moxley will compete on the January 6 episode of .

As confirmed during the January 4 episode of AEW Dynamite, the Blackpool Combat Club stablemates will battle Top Flight (Dane & Darius Martin) in a tag team match. The Martin brothers have clashed with the Blackpool Combat Club a few times in recent weeks, and they’ll face a tall task when they take on the former world champions.

Plus, AEW Women’s World Champion Jamie Hayter and Britt Baker will face The Renegade Twins (Charlette & Robyn Renegade) ahead of their bout against Saraya and Toni Storm on the January 11 episode of AEW Dynamite.

Perro Peligroso (formerly known as Preston Vance) will also be in action, but his opponent was not named. Plus, fans will hear from the House of Black (Brody King, Buddy Matthews & Malakai Black).

The card for Friday’s episode of AEW Rampage is below:

  • Jon Moxley & Bryan Danielson vs. Top Flight
  • We’ll hear from House of Black
  • Perro Peligroso (Preston Vance) vs. TBA
  • Jamie Hayter & Britt Baker vs. Renegade Sisters
