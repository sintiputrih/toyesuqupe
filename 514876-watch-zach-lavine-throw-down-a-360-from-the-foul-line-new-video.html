Watch Zach LaVine Throw Down A 360 From The Foul Line

Zach LaVine is preparing to go back to back to back this February as the NBA's Dunk Contest champion and Andrew Wiggins has just given a sneak preview of what the Wolves are cooking up for the All-Star festivities.

After last year's epic showdown between LaVine and Aaron Gordon, nobody should be surprised by any dunk that either of these two freaks pull off but a 360 from the foul line is straight up bananas.

And anybody who says "his foot was on the line, it doesn't count" is missing the point. The dude did a 360 from FIFTEEN feet away from the basket, stop the madness.
