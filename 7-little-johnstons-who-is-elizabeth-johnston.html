7 Little Johnstons: Who Is Elizabeth Johnston?

Elizabeth Johnston is the second biological child of the Johnston family, after her brother, Jonah Johnston. TLC calls her "the family's social butterfly and a hard-working honors student."

Elizabeth's prom landed in 2020 and she missed it because of the coronavirus pandemic, according to Daily Soap Dish, so her family had a small party for her at home. She decided to go into nursing for her future and says she wants to be a pediatric nurse practitioner. We could see her being so good at this!

That ain't all for Elizabeth, though. The future nurse is dating boyfriend Brice Bolden. In fact, Elizabeth's two close friends, Carol and Dacey, gave him a solid interrogation, according to People, to make sure he was worthy of their girl. Happily, Brice passed with flying colors.

Elizabeth moved out of her parents' home in early 2020 with her sister, Anna Johnston, according to TV Shows Ace. In typical fashion, this was very exciting for the two young women and extremely emotional for parents, Trent and Amber. Like any reality TV star worth their salt, Elizabeth is very active on Instagram and her posts are awesome. She loves sharing pics of herself with Brice and her family, so even though Elizabeth has left the nest, family is still her top priority.
