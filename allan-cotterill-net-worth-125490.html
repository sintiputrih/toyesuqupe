About

Known by the stage name Jeremy Thirteenth, he is a founder and lead vocalist for the post-hardcore, gothic rock band Snow White's Poison Bite. They have album releases titled The Story of Kristy Killings and Featuring: Dr. Gruesome And The Gruesome Gory Horror Show.

Before Fame

He, Juuso Puhakka, Tuomo Korander and Teemu Leikas formed the band in 2006. He started off as just the rhythm Guitarist for the group before also incorporating clean vocals. In 2009, he took over lead vocalist duties.
