Amal Clooney Just Wore the Perfect Party-Girl Fit in NYC

Amal Clooney is known for her workwear style sensibilities, but last night, she proved that she also knows how to dress for off-duty fun.

The human rights lawyer was photographed getting dinner in New York City in a look that shed her usual serious attire. After the ivory shift dress and sleek trench coat that she had been wearing earlier that day, Clooney slipped into a slinky sequined top, worn loosely tucked into a pair of blue jeans with raw hems.

Other highlights of her look included a cropped black blazer, which she color-coordinated with a mini black handbag, and a pair of metallic gold pumps. She went big on the accessories, too, wearing dazzling drop earrings.

Gotham//Getty Images

Earlier in the afternoon, Clooney was spotted wearing another office-friendly dress, this time a black midi number with tan polka dots. She carried a black handbag and wore black pumps.

Raymond Hall//Getty Images

TIME magazine recently named Clooney as one of 12 extraordinary women who are working to achieve equality across the globe. She opened up about the honor in an interview with the outlet last month.

"With everything going on today, I want to have a good answer when they ask me what I was doing," she said.

"You have to have dogged determination, and you can't let go because the stakes are so high. You're coming up against people whose power and existence depends on them continuing to commit serious abuses, and so they're not going to give up. So we definitely can't give up on our side," she later added.
