Career

Having parents stuck in a similar profession can go a long way in determining your future goal. There’s no exception in the case of Amber Midthunder. Her parents being already established entertainers, partly influence her career choice.

Her father is an actor and a remarkable one at that, while her mother has a history in film and video directing. Amber Midthunder refused to be left behind, which is why she’s already an actress on her 10th birthday.

She got on the screen at age 9 with an action-drama film titled- The Home Coming Of Jimmy Whitecloud, alongside her father DavidMidthunder, Kevan Micheals, and Phil Becker in 2001

She was also on set the following year in this time a horror film titled, Deadly Species, and in 2004 she appeared in the short film, Reservation War Parties.

After this, Amber Midthunder went for a break that lasted for four years and returned with an appearance with a minor role in the TV comedy-drama film titled, Sunshine Cleaning. The film was directed by her mother, Angelique Midthunder, and produced in 2008. It had other top actors on set, the likes of Emily Blunt, Amy Adams, and Alan Arkin.

While in 2009, she starred as the young version of the main character in the horror-thriller film, Not Forgotten, alongside Paz Vega.

She went AWOL for the second time and returned to the popular TV series, Longmire, where she played the role of Lily Stillwater between 2012 and 2014. Ever since, she has maintained consistency in a fantastic career.

Amber Midthunder‘s career was becoming on the rise, and in 2016 she appeared in the Academy Award Nomination of action-crime drama film, Hell Or High Water. That same year she featured in the romantic crime drama series titled, Priceless alongside Bianca Santos and Joel Smallbone in the lead roles.

And in 2017, Amber Midthunder made her first in what will be her most famous role so far in her career with the role of Kerry Loudermilk in the television series, Legion which lasted from 2017 to 2019. Her role in the series increased her popularity ranking and wealth.

From there, she continued to make the rounds in films like Cameras (2018), Only Mine, and most recently, portrayed the role of Rosa Ortecho in the television series, Roswell, New Mexico, from 2019 to 2021.

Amber Midthunder will also appear in the upcoming film in her first voice acting project as the voice of BW in the TV series animation, Long Gone Gulch, which is currently in the post-production stage.
