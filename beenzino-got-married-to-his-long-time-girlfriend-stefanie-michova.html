Beenzino Got Married To His Long-Time Girlfriend Stefanie Michova

Beenzino, a South Korean rapper, recently married his fiancée Stefanie Michova, a model and actress located in Germany. The rapper announced the news on social media on August 5 by releasing a couple of photos of himself with Michova. He stated:

“I have breaking news. Beenzino and Stefanie Michova discreetly marry in great style in the Borough Office of Yongsan District. While filling out Beenzino’s marriage registration paperwork, Yongsan District Borough Office staff broke out in a cold sweat.”

Michova also uploaded several photos of herself wearing a white dress and clutching a bunch of flowers.

Everything you need to know about Beenzino’s girlfriend, Stefanie Michova

Stefanie Michova, as previously said, is a model and actress from Germany. The actress, who was born on June 8, 1991, has starred in several television ads for businesses such as Chabien Mask and MGM Casino. He has also starred in films such as Unbloomed, Hooked, and Key West. Michova resides in Los Angeles and regularly goes to Korea for business. Wilhelmina’s modeling agency presently represents her. Her modeling career began in 2015, when she signed with Dragon Heart Global.

In Santa Monica, California, Michova attended the Joanne Baron/D.W. Brown Premiere Acting School. Cool for the Summer, Good Boy, Rollercoaster, Love Is A Temple, Black Magic, Long Live A$AP, Uncommitted, and more music videos have included her. In 2017, she made her directorial and writing debut with the short film Rise and Shine. Stefanie is very active on Instagram, where she has about 274,000 followers.

Beenzino and Stefanie Michova’s relationship

He and Stefanie Michova, also known as Lim Sung-bin, have been dating since 2015 and were sighted together the previous year. Lim, on the other hand, revealed information about Michova in June 2015 when he shared a photo of the latter on social media. He requested everyone to make harsh comments in English instead of Korean when appearing on jTBC’s Witch Hunt in 2015, explaining that he had to translate them to Michova since she wanted to know them. He later went on KBS2’s Yoo Hee-Sketchbook yeol’s in 2016 and said that dating Michova transformed his personality, that it occurred during a period when he allowed fame to get to his ego, and that everything changed after meeting the latter. Lim said that Michova taught him to be modest and humble.

Lim once remarked that after meeting Stefanie, he started to like traveling and visiting airports. He depicted Michova as a rustic girl, saying,

“I didn’t fall in love with Stefanie at first sight, but she was incredibly beautiful to me on our third trip together.” She is extremely straightforward. I went to New York with her once and was having a good time shopping when Stefanie burst out crying and said, ‘I don’t know anything and I don’t even enjoy it.’ Shopping is exhausting.’ I was really taken aback. “I learned that not everything about her was what I had assumed.”
