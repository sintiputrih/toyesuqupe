Table of Biography

Ben Carlin is one of the YouTubers who Makes and posts YouTube videos related to Movies and more. Ben Carlin along with his brother work together to produce and post content about theories related to movies. This USA-based YouTube has gained quite some fan following over the last couple of years.

Early Life

Ben Carlin is currently 31 years old. He was born in the year 1989 and celebrates his birthday on October 25th every year. Carlin spent his Childhood in the United States with his brother Jonathan. His parents’ names are John Carlin and Mary Carlin. He has an older brother named Jonathan Louis Carlin who is 2 years older than him.

The two brothers grew up together and have formed a good bond with each other.

Education

He is certainly a well-educated person but the details about his early schooling and high school are not known yet. He attended Radford University where he pursued two degrees one in Business Marketing and another in Management. The educational details of his brother Jonathan are still a mystery.

Career and Professional Life

Ben and Jonathan started their YouTube journey from the year 2012. They started by posting random vlogs and then shifted to producing videos related to movies and series. Their YouTube channel ‘SuperCarlin Brothers’ has around 2.1 million followers and receives a minimum of 100k+ views on each of their videos.

Their first video is titled ‘ Super Carlin Brothers teaser’. The video was posted on 30 May 2012 and has received 130k views on the video. Their most-watched video is titled ‘The Pixar Theory’ which has been successful in gathering 10 million views. His other videos that discuss theories on the movie ‘Inside Out’ have also gathered 6 million+ views in both videos.

The thumbnail of one of his YouTube videos ( source: YouTube)

The pair a movie geeks and both are Potterheads and are known to make videos on YouTube quizzing each other on Harry Potter and have named the video series Ben vs Jonathan. They have posted 1085 videos to date and are regular to post their videos. Jonathan posts videos every Tuesday and he posts a video every Thursday.

All their videos begin with “Hey, brother!” J’s videos end with “I will see you in another life, brother” and his end with “I will see you on Tuesday”. The duo’s videos on movie franchises Star Wars, The Last Airbender, and other Pixar movies have been widely popular.
