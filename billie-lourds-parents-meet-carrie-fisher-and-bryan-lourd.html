Billie Lourds Parents: Meet Carrie Fisher and Bryan Lourd

Billie Catherine Lourd is an American actress. She is known for starring as Chanel #3 in the Fox horror-comedy series Scream Queens and for her roles in the FX horror anthology series American Horror Story. She also appears as Lieutenant Connix in the Star Wars sequel trilogy.

Meet Carrie Fisher and Bryan Lourd

Billie Lourd was born and raised by lovely parents, Carrie Fisher (mother) and Bryan Lourd (father). Her mom was an actress who passed away in the year 2016. His dad is a talent manager/agent. She has a sister named Ava (Adopted by her father).

Carrie Fisher and Bryan Lourd dated for only three years and never married back in the 1990s, said UK Daily Mail. Fisher, 60, died on December 27 in a California hospital, just days after she suffered a massive heart attack on a transatlantic flight.

Fisher’s death was announced in a statement by Billie Lourd, a 24-year-old actress. The following day, Debbie Reynolds, Fisher’s mother, also died.
