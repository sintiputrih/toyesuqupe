Charlotte Cope Death Cause: Facebook Shaming Leads To Suicide, Paramedic Littering

Charlotte Cope, a young paramedic, died after being publicly shamed for littering on Facebook. Keep learning more about her.

Charlotte Cope was a Rhondda Fawr valley woman from Gelli. In April 2020, she was discovered dead at her family’s home.

On December 21, 2021, a judicial investigation into her death was launched. It revealed a lot of information about the young lady.

Who Is Charlotte Cope?

Charlotte Cope Death Cause: Charlotte Cope was a young and well-loved paramedic. She worked for the Welsh Ambulance Service for almost two years.

The young lady was based in Gelli station. She had attended Plymouth University to train to become a paramedic.

After graduating in 2018, she was employed with the Welsh Ambulance Service.

Cope was in love with her profession. She was continuously working on the frontline even during the coronavirus crisis.

Charlotte has been described as a popular and charming person by her friends. She loved sports, the gym, and more than that, helping other people.

However, she continued to suffer from mental health at times.

Charlotte Cope Died In 2020: Facebook Shaming Leads To Her Suicide

Charlotte Cope’s death cause has been recorded as suicide. She took her own life on April 13, 2020.

Cope had struggled with mental health issues. She was suffering from an eating disorder and anxiety at the time of her death.

The day before she died, photos of her in uniform were uploaded on Facebook. It claimed she had been littering outside her home.

Later that day, Cope was pictured standing outside her car next to a pile of litter.

Cope had already apologized for her mistakes. She was also ready to pay whatever fine was issued.

By the time she woke up the next day, the photographs had been shared hundred times on social media.

Sadly, she was found unconscious by her mother in her bedroom. Despite support from paramedics, she died inside the ambulance.

Her health condition was exacerbated by the photographs, which is perhaps, the reason behind her sudden death.

Charlotte Cope Was In Her Twenties: Her Age & Birthday

Charlotte Cope died at the age of 23 years. She was born in the year 1998 to parents Heidi and Roger Cope.

According to her parents, she suffered from an eating disorder since the age of 16.

Her family says that the photos shared on Facebook had humiliated Cope. They believe the posts have contributed to Charlotte’s death.
