About

Actress and Comedian who became well known for her starring role in the Netflix original series Love. She had previously been seen in Inside Amy Schumer, a Sketch series in which she was also a Writer.

Before Fame

In 2009, she won the Melbourne Fringe Best Comedy Award. Her debut show Monsters of the Deep 3D won the Brisbane Comedy Festival Award that same year.

Trivia

She co-wrote the book 100 Facts About Pandas with David O'Doherty and Mike Ahern. 

Family Life

Her father Chris O'Doherty performed with a band called Mental as Anything under the stage name Reg Mombassa. She has a sister named Lucy O'Doherty.

Associated With

She played the roommate of Gillian Jacobs character on the series Love.
