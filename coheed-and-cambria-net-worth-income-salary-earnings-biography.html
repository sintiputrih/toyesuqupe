Coheed and Cambria Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Coheed and Cambria is a 1995 Nyack, New York-based progressive rock band. Progressive rock, pop, heavy metal, and post-hardcore influences may be found in the group’s music. All of Coheed and Cambria’s albums are concept albums that chronicle The Amory Wars, a science fiction tale created by lead vocalist Claudio Sanchez that has been adapted into a series of comic books and a full-length novel. Seven studio albums, three live albums, and a number of limited-edition releases have been issued by the band. Velvet Hammer Music and Management Group, run by David “Beno” Benveniste, now manages them.
