Colin Kaepernick was born on November 3, 1987 in Milwaukee, WI. He is a dual-threat quarterback who rose to fame when he took over for Alex Smith mid-season in 2012 for the San Francisco 49ers. He then went on to lead his team to an NFC title, and was rewarded with a 6-year extension, worth over $110 million, in June 2014.

About

Dual-threat quarterback who took over for Alex Smith mid-season in 2012 for the San Francisco 49ers, and then went on to lead his team to an NFC title. The 49ers signed him to a 6-year extension, worth over $110 million, in June 2014.

Before Fame

He became the first NCAA Division 1 quarterback to throw for over 10,000 yards and rush for over 4,000 yards while starring at the University of Nevada, Reno. 

Trivia

He led the 49ers to a dramatic victory over Drew Brees and the New Orleans Saints in the second round of the 2012 NFL playoffs. 

Family Life

He was adopted by Teresa and Rick Kaepernick. 

Associated With

Head coach Jim Harbaugh helped bring a new winning order to San Francisco with the help of Kaepernick.
