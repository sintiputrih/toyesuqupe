Cooper Roth Biography

Cooper Roth is an American actor, well known for his portrayal as David in the ABC’s situation comedy Back in the Game and as voice of B-Dawg in Super Buddies. Roth got interest in acting when he was four year old and began to work in community theater and school plays. Cooper has a younger brother, Duncan Roth.
