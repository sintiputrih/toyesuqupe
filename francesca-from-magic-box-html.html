Francesca from Francesca and Leah - Age, Family, Bio

One of two stars of the YouTube toy channel Francesca and Leah alongside her sister. They have earned more than 2.1 million subscribers thanks to videos such as "CAKE SWITCH UP CHALLENGE!!!" and "Making Slime With Balloons!!! DIY Slime Balloon Tutorial!!!"

Before Fame

Her collaborative channel was originally launched in January of 2015.

Trivia

Her channel was renamed from Magic Box Toys Collector to simply Magic Box. Along with their popular slime videos, the channel has featured daily routines, vlogs, sports activities and challenges.

Family Life

She and her sister Leah shared the YouTube channel as well as an Instagram account and TikTok channel.

Associated With

She created content with her sibling like RonaldOMG and GamerGirl.
