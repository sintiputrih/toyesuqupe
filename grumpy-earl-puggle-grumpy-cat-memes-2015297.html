Grumpy Earl the Puggle Gives Grumpy Cat a Run for Her Money: Memes!

Time to get off the litter box, because there’s a new annoyed animal in town. Grumpy Earl the Puggle is quickly gaining Internet fame for his cranky mug and furrowed brow on Facebook.

His profile reads: “My name is Grumpy Earl. I am a puggle with the face of an 80-year-old man. I hate everyone and everything.”

The page already has thousands of likes and features a series of Earl’s funny memes. “If you’re happy and you know it, I don’t care,” one reads, while another quips, “Life is like a box of chocolates. I can’t eat chocolate.” 

Earl has quickly been dubbed “Grumpy Dog” after the Internet sensation “Grumpy Cat,” aka Tardar Sauce, hit the scene in 2012.
