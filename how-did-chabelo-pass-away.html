How did Chabelo pass away?

Mexican comedian, Chabelo who was known to be a great entertainer for kids has sadly passed away. According to his family, he died due to abdominal complications.

His death was reported today ( Saturday, March 25, 2023) and it was also revealed that he died earlier in the day.

“This is a very sad morning, Xavier Lopez Chabelo, father, brother, and husband, has left us suddenly due to abdominal complications,” the statement read.

“With a broken soul and knowing that many people have loved him for many years and that they will feel his loss, we ask you to pray for his rest and allow us to mourn peacefully the grief that overwhelms our entire family,” the family said.

He was known for being a children’s music singer who worked on tv for over 60 years and participated in more than thirty (30) motion pictures and also recorded over thirty (30) musical albums.

Chabelo also hosted the Sunday morning show dubbed En Familia con Chabelo for 48 straight years before the time of his death.
