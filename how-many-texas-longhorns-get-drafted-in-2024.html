How many Texas Longhorns get drafted in 2024?

The 2024 NFL Draft proved to be a historic milestone for the Texas Longhorns, as they shattered records and showcased their dominance in the college football landscape.

With an exceptional showing in the draft, the Longhorns have cemented their status as a force to be reckoned with in the college football arena. Let's delve into the details of the remarkable performance by the Texas Longhorns in the 2024 NFL Draft.

Unprecedented Success in the Draft

The Texas Longhorns left an indelible mark in the 2024 NFL Draft by having a staggering 11 players drafted across seven rounds, setting a new school record for the modern draft era. 

Noteworthy Selections

The Longhorns' success in the 2024 NFL Draft was highlighted by standout selections in various rounds. Notable picks included defensive lineman T'Vondre Sweat, defensive lineman Byron Murphy, wide receiver Xavier Worthy, running back Jonathon Brooks, and several others who were chosen by esteemed NFL teams.

    • Round 1: The Longhorns made an immediate impact in the first round with Byron Murphy being selected at No. 16 by the Seattle Seahawks, followed by Xavier Worthy at No. 28 by the Kansas City Chiefs.
  • Round 2: T'Vondre Sweat was a key addition, going at No. 38 to the Titans, followed by other impactful selections.
  • Round 4: The Longhorns secured the first pick of the fourth round, with the Panthers selecting tight end Ja'Tavion Sanders.
  • Round 5 and 6: The momentum continued with three players being drafted in Round 5 and two in Round 6, culminating in a total of 11 Longhorns finding their new NFL homes.

Impact of the Draft Record

The significance of this record-breaking performance cannot be overstated. The Texas Longhorns have demonstrated their ability to develop and nurture top-tier talent, contributing significantly to the NFL player pool.

This exceptional achievement not only reflects the prowess of the Longhorns' football program but also solidifies their position as a prominent source of pro-level talent.

Future Prospects and Returning Production

Despite the exodus of talent to the NFL, the Longhorns have shown remarkable resilience. As per the SP+ Rankings, the team still possesses the second-most returning production for the 2024 season in the SEC, indicating a promising outlook for the upcoming college football season.

The Longhorns' ability to maintain a high level of talent and production sets the stage for continued success and competitiveness in the collegiate football landscape.

Coach and Player Perspectives

Texas Longhorns coach Steve Sarkisian expressed his confidence in the drafted players' potential for growth and success in the NFL.

His insights shed light on the dedication and development of the players during their time with the Longhorns, emphasizing their versatility and promising future in professional football.

 

All in all, the Texas Longhorns' remarkable performance in the 2024 NFL Draft stands as a testament to the program's commitment to excellence and the cultivation of top-tier talent.

With a record-breaking number of players drafted, the Longhorns have solidified their position as a powerhouse in college football, and their impact on the NFL's player pool is undeniable.

To conclude, The Texas Longhorns' dominance in the 2024 NFL Draft marks a historic achievement that reinforces their legacy as a prolific source of NFL-caliber talent and sets the stage for an exciting future in college football.
