Douglas Theodore “Doug” Pinnick (born September 3, 1950), often known as dUg Pinnick or just dUg, is an American musician best known for his work with the hard rock/progressive metal band King’s X as a bass guitarist, songwriter, and co-lead vocalist. He’s released fifteen albums with King’s X, four solo albums, plus a slew of side projects and guest appearances. Pinnick’s gospel-like voice and extremely distorted bass tone, which he achieves with numerous Ampeg SVT-4 amplifiers, are well-known.
