Is Auston Matthews Mexican? Here's The Truth We Know About The Ice Hockey Player

Is Auston Matthews Mexican? Auston Taylour Matthews, a 24-year-old American professional ice hockey player, serves as the backup captain for the NHL’s Toronto Maple Leafs.

Matthews was a product of the USA Hockey National Team Development Program in the United States Hockey League before being chosen by the NHL in 2016 and competing for the ZSC Lions of the Swiss National League (USHL). He won the Swiss Cup in the same year.

Matthews, who was thought to be the top prospect, was selected first overall by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the draft. By being the first player in NHL history to start his career with four goals, he created history.

His efforts earned him the Calder Memorial Trophy as the best rookie in the NHL for the season. With 41 goals in 52 games, Matthews was the league’s top scorer in 2020–21, earning the Maurice “Rocket” Richard Trophy. 

Matthews hit the 60-goal plateau for the first time in 2021–22, broke records for the most goals scored by an American-born player and a Maple Leaf in a single season, and earned the Rocket Richard Trophy for the second consecutive year.

In addition to competing in two U18 World Championships, two U20 World Championships, and one IIHF World Championship, Matthews has also represented the United States internationally on several occasions.
