Rage became an integral member of the Outlaw Brothers, and under the leadership of Shahkim, they inked a production deal with the L.A. Posse.

Lady Of Rage Arrest Charges and Mugshot 

Contrary to any rumors or misinformation, there is no available information on The Lady of Rage being arrested or facing any charges.

Her record remains clear, focusing on her illustrious career in music and acting.

In 2017, The Lady of Rage faced health challenges that led to multiple hospitalizations.

Initially diagnosed with pneumonia due to experiencing shortness of breath, she later disclosed her battle with a “mystery” illness.

The condition was eventually identified as Sjogren’s Syndrome, an autoimmune disorder affecting moisture-producing glands in the body.

Symptoms include dry eyes, dry mouth, and fatigue, with potential complications in various organs.

The revelation of her health struggles sheds light on the resilience The Lady of Rage exhibited in the face of adversity.

Despite grappling with Sjogren’s Syndrome, a condition that can significantly impact one’s quality of life, she has continued her involvement in the music and acting industries.

Remarkably, she has also embraced motherhood, raising three children.

Lady Of Rage family details
