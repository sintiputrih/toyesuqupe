Jane Erin Carrey

Jane Erin Carrey is well-known as the only daughter of Jim Carrey, a well-known actor, and comedian. She is a singer, songwriter, actor, and producer who rose to prominence after applying for the 11th season of “American Idol.” Despite the fact that being removed was a big blow, she chose to follow her musical passion. She later became the lead singer for her own band, “The Jane Carrey Band.”

As a playback singer, she worked alongside her father on his biography film “Jim Carrey: I Needed Color” in 2017. Furthermore, two of her songs were featured in her father’s film “Dumb and Dumber To.” Living in the shadow of a comedic genius may have made it tough for Jane to carve out her own identity, but she always had her father’s complete support in anything she accomplished.
