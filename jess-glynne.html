Jessica Hannah Glynne is an English singer and songwriter. She is better known for signing with Atlantic Records. In 2014, she rose to fame after featuring on the singles ‘Rather Be’ by Clean Bandit’, ‘My Love’, and many others.

Additionally, Jessica Hannah Glynne aka Jess Glynne was considered one of the ‘Most Influential People Under 30 by Forbes magazine in 2019.

Career

Glynne completed her one-month crash music course at Access to Music London, where she met her future collaborators: songwriter Jin Jin and producer Bless Beats.

The most talented star Jess Glynne became famous for her song named ‘My Love’ which was released in the year 2013. In 2014, the song was ranked number one on the UK Singles Chart.

After its release, Glynne gained much appreciation as her song topped the UK charts, becoming the third fastest-selling single and most-streamed song of the year 2014. It was also named among the top ten of the US Billboard Hot 100.

Moreover, her Best British Single, “Rather Be” and “My Love” received nominations. Glynne was honored with the Grammy Award for Best Dance Recording with “Rather Be” and also earned a nomination for Song of the Year at the inaugural BBC Music Awards.

Achievements

Jess Glynne is the recipient of innumerable awards and nominations. Among many others, she has won The A&R Awards for Breakthrough Artist Award in 2016.
