Jowell - Age, Family, Bio

One half of the Puerto Rican reggaeton duo Jowell & Randy alongside Randy Acevedo. Also a solo artist, he released his debut album, The Pre-Season, in 2015.

Before Fame

Jowell & Randy first formed in 2000 but didn't release their first studio album until 2007 titled Casa de Leones.

Trivia

His YouTube channel Jowell Tv has become a popular outlet not only for his music videos but also for vlogs and other informal videos of his life.

Family Life

His real name is Joel Alexis Muñoz Martínez. He was born in Massachusetts but grew up in Puerto Rico.

Associated With

Both he and Yomo gained fame as reggaeton artists.
