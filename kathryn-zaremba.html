Kathryn Zaremba – Career and Professional Life

Kathryn Zaremba is best known for her roles as Annie Bennett Warbucks in the 1993 musical Annie Warbucks and Lisa Leeper in Full House.

Also, she co-starred in Bringing Up Jack and The Jeff Foxworthy Show, as well as making several appearances on Sisters.

This personality retired from acting when she was 13 years old. Moreover, during her time on Full House, she was a member of The Broadway Kids.

Eventually, she was cast as Annie Bennett Warbucks in the Broadway production of Annie Warbucks. And, Annie Warbucks received mixed reviews.

Moreover, the show never made it to Broadway and became an Off-Broadway production. Likewise, Annie Warbucks premiered at The Civic Theater in San Diego, California.

This personality works as an illustrator and surface designer. Further, she runs her business, Kate Zaremba Company in Washington, D.C. where she lives with her husband.

Kathryn sells her drawings, illustrations, and textiles under the name Kate Zaremba Company. Additionally, she also co-runs The Lemon Bowl.
