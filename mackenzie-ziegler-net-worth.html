Mackenzie Frances Ziegler Wiki Biography

Mackenzie Frances Ziegler was born on 4 June 2004, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania USA to Melissa Ziegler-Gisoni and Kurt Ziegler, and is best known as a dancer and a reality television star, particularly for appearing in ‘’Dance Moms’’.

So just how rich is Mackenzie Ziegler, as of early 2018? Authoritative sources report that Ziegler’s net worth is as high as $2 million, accumulated from her career as a dancer and her six years-long career as a reality television star. She earns around $600,000 per year for her dancing activities, in addition to acting, modelling and singing.

Mackenzie Ziegler Net Worth $2 million

Ziegler is currently in 8th grade, but is home-schooled, although also taking on-line classes. Mackenzie made her first ‘’Dance Moms’’ appearance in the episode entitled ‘’ The Competition Begins’’, originally aired on 13 July 2011. The series is focused on a group of dancing prodigies and the struggles they face on their way to the top. She has appeared in a total of 159 episodes of the series, making her last appearance in the episode entitled ‘’Maddie and Mackenzie Say Goodbye’’, in which the audience could see Mackenzie and her sister talk about their journey and experiences with the show. The series won the Kids’ Choice Award for Favorite Reality Show in 2013, then later the same year she starred in ‘’Dance Moms Christmas Special’’, all contributing significantly to Mackenzie’s net worth.

In 2014, she released the song ‘’Shine’’ as Mack Z, which she sang in ‘’Good Day New York’’ as of the same year, and her album ‘’Mack Z’’ took first place on the iTunes pop charts, then achieved worldwide success.

Mackenzie made her modeling debut in 2015, walking for the Polo Ralph Lauren Runway in New York City. Shortly after that, she started a campaign video for Glamour Magazine, so her modelling no doubt adds to her net worth.

In 2016, Mackenzie worked on several other projects, including ‘’Dance Moms: The Girls’ Guide to Life’’, and ‘’So You Think You Can Dance’’, appearing in three episodes. Besides that, the audience could see Ziegler as a guest-star in the television series ‘’JoJo’s Juice’’, also in three episodes.

In 2016, she earned a Teen Choice Award nomination for the Choice Muser, voted by 13-19 year-old viewers, and released her first single entitled ‘’Day and Night’’ under her real name in a duet with Johnny Orlando, and   appeared in its music video. As of her most recent work, she released the song ‘’Teamwork’’ in 2017, and the song ‘’Monsters Aka Haters’’. Overall, she has already appeared in 40 music videos, television shows and movies.

Ziegler and her sister were spokespeople for Clean & Clear cosmetics, and she has also launched a clothing line of activewear inspired by dancing, through retailer Justice.

When it comes to Mackenzie’s personal life, her parents, Melissa and Kurt are divorced. Her older sister, Maddie, is a dancer as well. At just 13, she has yet to experience the joys of romance, but is active on social media, known for using the Musical.ly app on which she has over eight million followers. She plans to release a self-help book in 2018.

Mackenzie is widely known for her charity work too – she, her mother and sister have worked with the Starlight Children’s Foundation, among others including Dancers Against Cancer, and Love Your Melon.
