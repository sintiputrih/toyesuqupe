Dr. Michael Conrad: Remembering a Dedicated Obstetrician-Gynecologist

Dr. Michael Conrad, an obstetrician-gynecologist based in Huntsville, Alabama, passed away on Monday, July 17, 2023. His untimely demise has left the medical community mourning the loss of an exceptional physician, compassionate caregiver, and influential figure in the field of women's health. Let us take a moment to reflect on the life and legacy of Dr. Conrad, a remarkable individual who touched the lives of many.

1. Introduction

In this article, we commemorate the life and contributions of Dr. Michael Conrad, a highly regarded obstetrician-gynecologist known for his dedication and expertise in the medical field. Dr. Conrad's impact extended beyond his practice, as he played a vital role in advancing robotic surgery techniques and tirelessly advocated for women's health.

2. Early Life and Education

Dr. Michael James Conrad pursued his medical education at The University of Tennessee, College of Medicine. He exhibited exceptional talent and passion for the field, which paved the way for his remarkable career. After completing medical school, Dr. Conrad pursued his residency at St. John's Mercy Medical Center, where he honed his skills and gained invaluable experience.

3. Medical Career and Achievements

3.1 Practice and Experience

Dr. Conrad dedicated over 20 years to his medical practice, with more than a decade spent serving the Huntsville community. His commitment to his patients and his unwavering pursuit of excellence made him a trusted figure in women's health. Dr. Conrad's expertise and compassionate care positively impacted the lives of countless individuals.

3.2 Pioneering Robotic Surgery

Dr. Michael Conrad's contributions to the field of obstetrics and gynecology reached new heights when he became the first physician in Huntsville to undergo master's training in robotic surgery. He performed numerous procedures using the daVinci robot at Crestwood Medical Center and Huntsville Hospital. In fact, for several years, Dr. Conrad conducted over 100 robotic surgeries annually, showcasing his expertise in this cutting-edge surgical technique.

3.3 Professional Affiliations

Apart from his practice, Dr. Conrad held esteemed positions in the medical community. He served as the Director of Robotics and the Director of Women's and Children's at Crestwood Medical Center, where he provided leadership and guidance to his colleagues. Additionally, Dr. Conrad held the position of Adjunct Clinical Professor at several medical schools, further contributing to the education and training of future healthcare professionals. He also actively participated in various boards, including the Madison County Medical Society and "Lilies of the Valley."
