Biography

Naguib Sawiris was born in Egypt on June 15, 1954. Entrepreneur and political activist who headed the Orascom Telecom Holding (OTH) company in Egypt. He served on the board of the Supreme Council of Sciences and Technology, which was created under the mandate of Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak. He founded WIND Telecom in 2005 and in the same year, became a member of the New York Stock Exchange’s international advisory committee.
