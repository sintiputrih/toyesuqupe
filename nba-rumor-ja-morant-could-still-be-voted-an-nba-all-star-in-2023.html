NBA Rumor: Ja Morant Could Still be Voted an NBA All Star in 2023

The NBA All-Star Game is a highly anticipated event that brings together the best basketball players in the world. Every year, fans eagerly await the announcement of the players who will represent their respective conferences. However, there are times when deserving players are left off the initial roster due to the limited number of spots available. This has led to numerous rumors and debates about whether certain players should have been included. One such rumor revolves around Ja Morant, the talented young point guard for the Memphis Grizzlies. Despite not making the All-Star team in 2022, there is speculation that Morant could still receive the honor in 2023. In this article, we will explore the reasons behind this rumor and assess the likelihood of it becoming a reality.

Ja Morant's Rise to Stardom

To understand why there is a rumor surrounding Morant's potential All-Star selection in 2023, it is important to acknowledge his remarkable journey in the NBA so far. After being selected as the second overall pick in the 2019 NBA Draft, Morant wasted no time making an impact on the court. He quickly established himself as one of the most exciting young players in the league, showcasing his exceptional athleticism, court vision, and scoring ability.

During his rookie season, Morant averaged an impressive 17.8 points, 7.3 assists, and 3.9 rebounds per game. His stellar performances earned him the NBA Rookie of the Year award, solidifying his status as a rising star in the league. Morant continued to excel in his second season, averaging 19.5 points, 7.7 assists, and 4.0 rebounds per game. He played a crucial role in leading the Grizzlies to the playoffs, further enhancing his reputation as one of the league's brightest young talents.

The NBA All-Star Selection Process

The NBA All-Star selection process involves a combination of fan voting, player voting, and media voting. The fans account for 50% of the vote, while current NBA players and a select group of media members each account for 25% of the vote. The players and media members vote for the players within their respective conferences, while the fans vote for players from both conferences. The top vote-getters in each conference are named as the starters, while the coaches select the reserves.

In the 2022 NBA All-Star Game, Morant did not receive enough votes from fans, players, or media members to earn a spot on the roster. Despite his impressive performances, there was intense competition in the guard position in the Western Conference, with players like Stephen Curry, Damian Lillard, and Chris Paul securing the available spots.

Reasons for the Rumor

There are several reasons why there is a rumor that Morant could still be voted an NBA All-Star in 2023. Firstly, Morant's popularity and reputation have continued to grow since his rookie season. He has become a fan favorite, known for his electrifying dunks and clutch performances. As his popularity rises, so does his chances of receiving more votes from fans in the All-Star selection process.

Secondly, Morant's on-court performances have improved year after year. He has shown significant development in his game, particularly in terms of his shooting and decision-making. As he continues to elevate his performance, it is likely that his fellow players and the media will take notice and give him the recognition he deserves.

Lastly, there is often a level of unpredictability in the All-Star selection process. While the fans, players, and media members have their preferences, there have been instances where deserving players have been snubbed, leading to public outcry. The NBA acknowledges this and has implemented a system where the commissioner can select injury replacements for players who are unable to participate in the All-Star Game. This system provides an opportunity for players like Morant, who may have narrowly missed out on the initial selection, to still be considered.

Likelihood of Morant Becoming an All-Star in 2023

While the rumors surrounding Morant's potential All-Star selection in 2023 hold some merit, it is important to consider the competitive landscape of the NBA. The guard position in both conferences is stacked with talented players, making it challenging for any player to secure a spot. Additionally, the fan voting process can be influenced by the popularity of certain players, which may not always align with their on-court performance.

However, Morant's continued growth as a player, coupled with his increasing popularity, certainly improves his chances of being voted an All-Star in the future. If he maintains his upward trajectory and continues to make a significant impact for the Grizzlies, it is conceivable that he could garner enough votes from fans, players, and media members to secure a spot on the All-Star roster.

The rumor surrounding Ja Morant's potential All-Star selection in 2023 stems from his impressive performances, growing popularity, and the inherent unpredictability of the All-Star selection process. While it is uncertain whether Morant will ultimately become an All-Star in 2023, it is clear that he has the talent and potential to compete at the highest level. As fans eagerly await the announcement of the 2023 NBA All-Star roster, the possibility of Morant's inclusion adds to the anticipation and excitement surrounding the event.
