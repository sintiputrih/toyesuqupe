Nick Carter Reveals Daughters Name In Heartfelt Message To Wife Lauren Kitt

Nick Carter revealed the name of his daughter in a heartfelt message to his wife, Lauren Kitt.

One week after his second child and first daughter was born, Carter is using social media to share a little more information about the baby. The Backstreet Boy singer praised his wife, in the same post that also featured the first photo of their baby girl.

“To my incredibly strong wife…For 2 years, I watched you endure some of the hardest things a husband could ever witness. Going through the ups and downs of pregnancy all in the hopes that just maybe, with a little help from up above, we would get to this point,” Carter began his lengthy message.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B3QV8ctBPJG/

It has been one year since Lauren suffered a miscarriage. The couple shared the news last fall that they had lost a baby girl when Lauren was three months pregnant.

Sadly, the loss was not the first miscarriage Kitt suffered. She also revealed before having their son Odin Reign that Kitt suffered a miscarriage, also a little girl.

Thankfully all the devastation is behind the proud parents. Carter went on to reveal the day he found out his wife was pregnant with his daughter.

“I’ll never forget the moment You asked me what I wanted for my birthday, I said nothing. But deep down inside I knew what I really wanted. So, on January 28th 2019, when I walked out of that bathroom with that positive test I already knew that Saoirse was coming back to us. I just want to thank you for giving me the Life, Love, and family that I always wanted. You are a true Warrior, and I love you. #mommy #freedom #newbaby #saoirse #saoirsereigncarter #family,” the proud husband and father finished his message.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B3Zp95Dhfs3/

In the hashtag section of his post, Carter revealed his daughter’s name is Saoirse Reign Carter. The little bundle of joy was born on October 2nd. She joins big brother, 3-year-old Odin.

Nick Carter shared a powerful message to his wife, Lauren Kitt, following the birth of their daughter. The same day he sang her praises, the shared their first photo of Saoirse in a new US Weekly spread. They discuss their family’s journey from devastation to pure bliss, as Nick cradles the little girl.
