PGA Tour Superstore snags space for first store in Charlotte WSOC TV

CHARLOTTE — PGA Tour Superstore has landed its first store in North Carolina.

The specialty retailer for golf and tennis has signed a lease for a 35,640-square-foot location at Promenade on Providence in south Charlotte.

It will be at 5341 Ballantyne Commons Parkway, Suite 200.

The store is expected to open in spring 2022.
