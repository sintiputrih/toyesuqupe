Rihanna and Drake Step Out Together for Fourth Night in a Row

Are these two hiding in plain sight? Rihanna and Drake stepped out to a club in London on Friday, July 1, their fourth sighting together in the last week amid rumors that the former lovers are back on again.

Rihanna, 28, wore an eye-popping all-blue denim ensemble for the night, with her hair swept up in her trademark high ponytail.

Drake, 29, kept his look casual in dark slacks and an army green jacket. The pair hung out together at Tape nightclub.

Earlier this week, Rihanna helped celebrate her pal Nicole Scherzinger’s birthday with a surprise bash at Tape nightclub in London on Tuesday, June 28. Drake was also in attendance.

On Wednesday, June 29, Drake joined the “This Is What You Came For” singer on stage for her Manchester show.

“The most beautiful, talented woman I’ve ever seen in my life,” Drake praised Rihanna after the pair performed her hit “Work” on Wednesday, June 29, for the excited crowd. “She goes by the name of Rihanna. … I’m getting my heart broken over here.”

The “Hotline Bling” singer then gave the Grammy winner a huge hug as the audience cheered.

Afterward, the pair headed to Tramps nightclub together and were spotted leaving around 4:30 a.m., just seconds apart.

And on Thursday, the twosome both donned black for an outing to another London nightclub and were photographed returning to her London hotel around 4 a.m.

The two have been linked since February 2014 and have been spotted getting cozy in recent months.
