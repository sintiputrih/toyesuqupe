Rihanna Leaves Aspen in a Fuzzy Leopard-Print Coat and Sweatsuit

Pink puffers, knee-length letterman jackets, heart-shaped mini coats—Rihanna is a fashion icon, and one of the areas where she particularly thrives is outerwear.

Most recently, the Super Bowl–headlining superstar was spotted at the airport in Aspen with her beau, A$AP Rocky. Boarding a plane, RiRi was seen in yet another eye-popping piece of outerwear, this time courtesy of Giorgio Armani. On the tarmac, the “Diamonds” singer was photographed in a hooded faux fur long coat in leopard print, which she kept unzipped, revealing her matching Awake NY logo hoodie-and-sweatpants set, both in a parchment color with blue stitching underneath.

BACKGRID

Rihanna completed the fit with a pair of white faux fur Moon Boots decorated with brown laces and multicolored beads. She also sported a long, palm green beanie from Matek that featured a pair of thick black stripes.

The Ocean’s 8 actor is among the latest stars seen visiting Aspen; other celebs, like Hailey Bieber and Kendall Jenner, recently made their own trips to the Colorado wintertime ski destination. And although this latest look was captured while Rihanna was leaving town, she made plenty of fashion statements during her vacay. For instance, she was spotted in a steel-gray parachute parka and matching cargo pants from Balenciaga, which she paired with the brand’s fake mink low boots in black.

And while shopping in Aspen, the singer wore an oversize single-breasted cream blazer from Yves Saint Laurent, which she paired with light-wash distressed Balenciaga jeans and a cashmere hoodie from New Bottega.
