What is Rocco Mediate's Net Worth?

Rocco Mediate is an American professional golfer who has a net worth of $20 million. Rocco Mediate was born in Greensburg, Pennsylvania in December 1962. He golfed at Florida Southern College and turned professional in 1985. Mediate has competed on the PGA Tour and Champions Tour and has 12 professional wins in his career including six on the PGA Tour and 2 on the Champions Tour. His best major result was finished 2nd at the U.S. Open in 2008.

Mediate finished 6th at the PGA Championship in 2002. He tied for 15th at the 2001 Masters Tournament and tied for 18th at the 1996 The Open Championship. He finished 2nd to Tiger Woods at the 2008 U.S. Open after losing in sudden death. Mediate has won the Doral-Ryder Open in 1991, the Kmart Greater Greensboro Open in 1993, the Phoenix Open in 1999, the Buick Open in 2000, the Greater Greensboro Chrysler Classic in 2002, and the Frys.com Open in 2010.
