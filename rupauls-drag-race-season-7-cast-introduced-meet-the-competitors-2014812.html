RuPaul's Drag Race Season 7 Cast Introduced: Meet the Competitors

It’s on! RuPaul is back at it with another group of competing queens. 

The actor, singer, and famed drag queen debuted the new cast for the next season of RuPaul’s Drag Race on Sunday, Dec. 7. The host, 54, was joined by 14 new competitors for the upcoming seventh season of the Logo TV show.  

“Me? I’m just a humble gal with a reality show,” RuPaul says in a new promo for the show. “This season’s queens are a pretty straightforward bunch… These ladies have nothing to hide… and that’s all there is to it.” 

The new stars are then introduced as various competitors describe themselves as “a real bitch,” someone who is afraid of “youth and beauty,” and those who are not intimidated by fierce competition. 

“This year we have some of the youngest and oldest contestants in RuPaul’s Drag Race herstory,” RuPaul said in a statement. “Study each queen closely. Do not underestimate anyone. The girls that make it to the top this season will shock you.”
