Ryan Phillippe Children: Meet Ava Elizabeth Phillippe, Deacon Reese Phillippe and Kai Knapp

Ryan Phillippe is an American actor and the father of three children Ava Elizabeth Phillippe, Deacon Reese Phillippe and Kai Knapp.

Kai Knapp was born on July 1, 2011. She is 11 years old. Ava Phillippe was born on September 9, 1999. She is 23 years old. Ava is a senior at the University of California, Berkeley.

It’s also clear that Ava is a talented artist. On social media, she posts sketches of people, animals, and abstracts. Ava also dabbles in clay sculpture, producing extremely realistic renderings.

Ava became the face of another fashion brand after making her Rodarte debut: her mother, Reese Witherspoon’s clothing and lifestyle line, Draper James.

Ava Phillippe has been dating Owen Mahoney since around 2019. They met at UC Berkeley, where they are both currently enrolled in undergrad.

Deacon Reese Phillippe was born on October 23, 2003. He is 18 years old. Deacon is an aspiring actor.

Deacon made his acting debut in Never Have I Ever’s third instalment, which was produced by Mindy Kaling. He as he also recently released an eight-song album on SoundCloud, titled Just a Kid.
