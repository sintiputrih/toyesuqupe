Sean Penn Dyes His Hair Blonde Like Girlfriend Charlize Theron's: Pic

His and her hair! Sean Penn stepped out on Saturday, Nov. 29, sporting new blonde locks just like his girlfriend Charlize Theron‘s.

Rocking a plaid shirt and his new lighter disheveled ‘do, the 54-year-old actor helped his lady love, 39, and her 2-year-old son Jackson with groceries in Beverly Hills, Calif.

The couple, who have been dating almost a year, spent the past two months together in South Africa, where they’ve been filming upcoming drama The Last Face.
