Shane Beamer Children: Who Are Shane Beamers Kids?

Shane Beamer is an American football coach who is currently serving as an assistant head coach as well as the tight end coach at the famed University of Oklahoma. He was born on March 31, 1977. He is the son of long-time Virginia Tech head coach, Frank Beamer.

Beamer was a part of the staff that coached the nation-leading scoring offence in 2018, where they averaged 48.4 points per game.

On December 6, 2020, University of South Carolina director, Ray Tanner announced that the school hired Beamer as its head coach.
