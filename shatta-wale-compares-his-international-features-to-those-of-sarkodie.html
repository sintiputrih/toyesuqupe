Shatta Wale Compares His International Features To Those Of Sarkodie

Self-acclaimed dancehall king of Africa, Shatta Wale has put the spotlight on his International features and that of his fellow musician, Sarkodie.

It is now obvious Shatta Wale is bent on proving he has a superior record than Sarkodie at all costs.
