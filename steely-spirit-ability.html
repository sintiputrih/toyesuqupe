Steely Spirit

Flavor text

Generation IX
Powers up the Steel-type moves of the Pokémon and its allies.

Steely Spirit (Japanese: はがねのせいしん Steely Spirit) is an Ability introduced in Generation VIII. It is the signature Ability of Perrserker.

Effect

In battle

The power of Steel-type moves used by a Pokémon with Steely Spirit and its allies is increased by 50%. This effect stacks if multiple Pokémon with this Ability are on the field.

Outside of battle

Steely Spirit has no effect outside of battle.
