Steve Lee - Age, Family, Bio

Singer and comedian also known as Quangou. He released tracks including "Goodbye Balloon" and "Rodeo Man." He is also known for hosting The Steebee Weebee podcast and co-hosting the Scissor Bros podcast with comedian Jeremiah Watkins. He has amassed more than 130,000 subscribers on his Steebee Weebee YouTube channel.

Before Fame

He attended Poway High School in Poway, California. He wrestled in high school and qualified twice for the state championships. He attended Arizona State University. He spent decades working in the customer service industry.
