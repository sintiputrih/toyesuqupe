Terry Forrester Tragically Passes Away in Ooltewah Accident

In a heart-wrenching incident, Terry Forrester, a 54-year-old individual, lost his life in a devastating crash in Ooltewah on a fateful Friday afternoon, according to Chattanooga Police reports.

The accident unfolded on Mountain View Road, where Terry Forrester was traveling northbound in his gray Ford Ranger. Tragically, a southbound white Honda Accord, driven by a 16-year-old, veered across the double yellow center line, resulting in a collision with Forrester’s vehicle.

Regrettably, Terry Forrester succumbed to his injuries as a result of the crash. The 16-year-old driver was transported to the hospital for the treatment of non-life-threatening injuries. The incident necessitated the closure of the roadway for approximately three hours to facilitate investigations and manage the aftermath, as reported by Chattanooga Police.
