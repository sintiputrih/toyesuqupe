The Devils You Know, Starfield Mission

1. Proceed to your meeting (Subsection Seven)

Return to the building and use the elevator to get to the previously inaccessible "Subsection Seven" level. Go forward until you reach a room where a man is sitting behind a glass wall.

2. Use the intercom

There's someone living down here... and he's asked to speak to me.

Use the intercom located on the table to start a conversation.

3. Speak to the stranger / Speak to Vae Victis

After conversing for a while, you will learn that the person you are talking to is Fran√ßois Sanon, known as Vae Victis. He has valuable information that may be useful to you but in return he wants you to do something for him.

4. Speak to Captain Marquez / (Optional) Speak to Deputy MacIntyre

I just spoke to Vae Victis, a former UC admiral and a man who's supposed to be dead. He asked me to track down - and "deal with" - a rogue weapons scientist named Reginald Orlase. He believes Orlase is hiding near the planet of Etherea in the Wolf system, and that I should speak to the local head of the Vanguard - Captain Marquez - to get specifics.

Before you continue on your way, you can talk to MacIntyre and tell her what you learned and what Vae Victis asked you to do. You will receive her full approval and a request for discretion.

Then return to your ship, travel to the Wolf system and dock at The Den space station.

Captain Marquez is aboard the space station. Ask her about Orlase and she will give you the coordinates of the ship he may be on.

Before you leave the station, take the "UC Defense Manual 02" magazine that you will find in one of the space station's rooms.

5. Find "The Warlock"

Captain Marquez has given me the coordinates I need to find "The Warlock," a ship that may be home to Orlase. But she warned me to be careful - a number of ships have been lost in the debris fields where the Warlock's been seen.

Return to your ship and proceed to the Etherea Ruins.

After a while you should be attacked by an enemy ship.

6. Destroy the ship / Collect the evidence

I've found the Warlock. And whoever's piloting it doesn't sound pleased I'm here. Time to either disable his engines to capture him (using an EM Weapon or the Targeting Control Systems skill)... or shoot him out of the sky.

You can simply destroy the enemy ship and then take the UC Science Division ID: Orlase, Reginald from its remains as proof that you did it.

I destroyed the Warlock - and its pilot with it. I need to search the wreckage, find evidence this was Orlase.

7. OR Disable the engines / Board "The Warlock" / Arrest the pilot / Collect the evidence

You can also disable the engines of an enemy ship, which will enable you to board it.

I've disabled the Warlock's engines. Now I should be able to board his ship and see if I can't capture its pilot!

I'm onboard the Warlock! Time to confront the pilot!

Orlase has locked himself in the cockpit and you won't be able to arrest him because he will choose to kill himself rather than get arrested.

Orlase killed himself rather than be captured. I guess I should check him for evidence I could show to Vae Victis.

Just wait a while for the emergency opening of the cockpit after the pilot's death and take the evidence from his corpse.

8. Return the evidence to Vae Victis

I found Orlase's old United Colonies ID. This should be sufficient evidence to prove to Vae Victis the job is done.

9. Transfer the ID to Vae Victis

Vae Victis has asked me to hand over the evidence I collected that Orlase is dead. I should approach the transfer system.

10. Speak to Vae Victis

I've handed over the ID. Time to get the location of Hadrian and Percival's research team.

Once again speak with Vae Victis. He will give you the information you need after which this mission will end and another one will begin - "War Relics". You will receive 150 XP and 3,000 credits.

Vae Victis is going to arrange to have the research team arrive at the Red Devils HQ. All but one - Kaiser - who I'll need to recover from Niira. Vae Victis suggested I check in with Hadrian first, as she'll likely have more information about this missing teammate.
