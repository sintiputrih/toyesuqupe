Tony Stewart Net Worth 2024

Tony Stewart is a NASCAR star known as Smoke, who was born on May 20, 1971 in Columbus, IN. He has achieved great success in the sport, winning the NASCAR Sprint Cup Series Championship three times in 2002, 2005, and 2011. He was also named Indianapolis 500 Rookie of the Year in 1996 and Winston Cup Series Rookie of the Year in 1999. In 2009, he became co-owner of Stewart-Hass Racing.
