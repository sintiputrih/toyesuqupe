VICTORIA Beckham looked stunning in a swimsuit and tiny shorts as she cosied up to David on their luxurious holiday.

The 49-year-old took to Instagram with her beau as they wished fans a happy Christmas.

Victoria donned a dark blue cutout swimsuit with ripped denim shorts, while she affectionately cuddled up to David.

David wore a brown straw hat, a light brown t-shirt, and dark brown rimmed sunglasses.

The couple posed in front of a drink with the backdrop of nature and trees.

David captioned the snap: "Merry Christmas to my amazing family & feeling very thankful for everything they do for me , love you all."

Fans were delighted by the reveal video Victoria posted on Instagram.

In it she zoomed in on the hen house, asking her husband: "What did you get for Christmas David?"
