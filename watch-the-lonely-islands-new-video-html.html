Watch the Lonely Islands New Video for Spring Break Anthem

Hey, fans of Internet things, the Internet has mashed up a few of your favorite things into one handy four-minute video. After one minute of Zach Galifianakis “Between Two Ferns”-ing James Franco, we get a full Lonely Island music video about sprang break. It’s part of Lonely Island’s “Wack Wednesdays,” where they’ll be releasing a new song or video every Wednesday until their new album, The Wack Album, comes out on June 11. See you next Wednesday, Lonely Island. Congrats on your weddings.
