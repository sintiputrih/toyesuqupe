What Happened To The Married At First Sight Season 15 Couples After The Show

Lifetime’s Married at First Sight made a comeback earlier this year with a rip-roaring fifteenth season. For the show’s historic West Coast debut, OG MAFS experts Dr. Pepper Schwartz and Pastor Cal Robertson teamed up with newcomers DeVon Franklin and Dr. Pia Holec to create five presumably unbeatable love matches out of the thousands of romantic hopefuls who’d hoped to join season 15’s cast lineup.

Following their sight unseen weddings and romantic honeymoons in the charming beaches of Puerto Vallarta, Mexico, season 15 couples got to move in together as they prepared to make the ultimate decision; stay married or get divorced. Here’s everything that’s happened since these monumental decisions were made and season 15 came to its inevitable conclusion.

8 Stacia Karcher And Nate Barnes Are Still Together

Season 15’s star couple Stacia Karcher and Nate Barnes are still going strong! That said, their relationship isn’t all sunshine and rainbows, with Nate still struggling to meet Stacia’s emotional needs.

“I do feel satisfied in moments,” Stacia disclosed during MAFS season 15 reunion special. “I just want it consistently, and like he said, I can be a moving target because I expect a lot.”

7 Alexis Williams And Justin Davis Have Split Up

Alexis and Justin were one of the three MAFS season 15 couples who decided to stay married on ‘Decision Day.’ However, the two got into a fierce argument shortly after filming the finale, bringing an end to their eight-week marriage.

“I felt like I was not free, and I’m a very free-spirited individual. And I felt like I was losing myself,” Alexis shared during the reunion special. “We still tried to be friends.”

6 Alexis And Justin’s Relationship Is Still As Volatile As Ever

Despite opting to remain friends, Alexis and Justin’s relationship is still as volatile as ever. During the reunion special, Justin revealed that Alexis had drunk-dialed him following their break-up, a claim which Alexis fervently denied.

Infuriated by his ex-wife’s response, Justin proceeded to call Alexis a “compulsive liar,” claiming that she was “doing that gaslighting thing,” and “trying to manipulate the truth.”

5 Miguel Santiago And Lindy Elloway Are Still Working On Their Relationship

Miguel and Lindy developed almost instantaneous romantic chemistry following their nuptials. As such, it came as no surprise when the couple decided to remain together on ‘Decision Day.’

That said, the duo’s marriage might not be as rosy as it appears, if a recent Instagram post, where Lindy was featured not wearing her wedding band is anything to go by.

4 Lindy And Miguel Finally Reached A Compromise On Last Names

While Lindy and Miguel’s marriage might not be perfect, the pair has certainly demonstrated their capability and willingness to communicate through their issues and compromise. The two even managed to compromise on last names.

“I feel so confident being your wife,” Lindy, who’d recently hyphenated her name, revealed during MAFS season 15 reunion, “so it was an easy decision for me when I realized I was ready to take your last name.”

3 Krysten Collins And Mitch Silverstein Have Not Rekindled Their Romance

Krysten and Mitch were one of the two season 15 couples whose marriage didn’t make it past filming. Although the two are trying to remain on good terms, Mitch’s decision to walk away from the marriage still stupefies Krysten.

“I truly didn’t think there was a chance that he wouldn’t be attracted to me,” she explained during the reunion. “I don’t think I’m Jessica Biel, but I do think I bring a lot to the table. So, I was a little surprised, and I didn’t really believe him.”

2 Has Krysten Made Peace With Mitch’s Decision To Get Divorced?

Despite being utterly dumbfounded, Krysten has made peace with Mitch’s decision to walk away from their eight-week marriage.

“I will never want to be with someone who is unsure about me or says things like ‘unlikely’ or ‘likely,’” she said during the reunion special. “I know what I’m worth and that ain’t it.”

1 Binh Trinh And Morgan Bell Are Still Good Friends

Morgan and Binh were the only season 15 couple who didn’t even make it to ‘Decision Day.’ While their marriage might have unraveled quite rapidly, their friendship seems fortified to a fault.

“At some points, we kind of talk, we kind of laugh, we’re like, ‘Oh my god, I cannot believe that all happened, I cannot believe we went through that,’” Morgan disclosed to Entertainment Tonight in October. “Because now we’re just completely different people at the end of the day, and it’s honestly wonderful.”
