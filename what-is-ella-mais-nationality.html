What is Ella Mai's nationality?

Ella Mai is a beautiful and famous singer and songwriter who has been active since 2014. Her musical career began at London’s British and Irish Modern Music Institute in 2014, during which time she auditioned as part of a trio on the 11th season of The X Factor.

She is a talented singer as far as her career is concerned and she is loved by many following her great personality. In this article, we will dive into her early life and origin to know where nationality.

What is Ella Mai’s nationality?

According to the information gathered, she is British.

She was born in  London, United Kingdom to a Jamaican mother and an English-Irish father. According to the singer, her mother, a lover of American jazz music, named her after Ella Fitzgerald.

As a singer, she has won several awards including Grammy Award for Best R&B Song, BET Award for Viewer’s Choice, and Billboard Music Award for Top R&B Artist, just to mention a few.
