Who Is Irina-Camelia Begu? Meet The Romanian Tennis Player On Instagram

Romanian Tennis player Irina-Camelia Begu’s biography has been featured on Wikipedia. Is she on Instagram? Let us learn more about her below!

Irina-Camelia Begu is a tennis player.

She has won four singles titles and nine doubles titles on the WTA Tour. Likewise, she also has won one singles title on the WTA 125K series, as well as 12 singles and 19 doubles titles on the ITF Circuit.

Who Is Irina-Camelia Begu? Explore Her Wikipedia & Biography

Irina- Camelia Begu, the tennis player’s biography has been featured on the official Wikipedia page.

She was birthed on August 26, 1990, in Bucharest, Romania. Her zodiac sign is Virgo. Talking about her physical attributes, she stands at the height of 5 feet 11 inches tall. In addition to that, she was born to Steluta, her mother who is a civil servant, and her father Paul who is an electrician.

She was reared with her older brother, Andrei in Bucharest. Moreover, the tennis player was first introduced to the sport by her aunt, former tennis player Aurelia Gheorghe and she began playing tennis when she was three-and-a-half years old.

Previously, she also did gymnastics and played handball. By the age of 14, she took part in a one-month tour promoted by the ITF with seven other girls and eight boys, joining the likes of Grigor Dimitrov and Ricardas Berankis.

She won the European 16 & Under title in 2006. Following that, she earned the ranking junior world No 14 on August 27, 2007, the day after she turned 17.

In May 2009, she won her first major ITF title at the $100K Bucharest, alongside Simona Halep. The same year, she made her WTA Tour doubles debut at the 2009 Palermo International.

Is Irina-Camelia Begu On Instagram?
