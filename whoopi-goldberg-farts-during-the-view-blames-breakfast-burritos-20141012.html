Whoopi Goldberg Farts During The View, Blames Breakfast Burritos

It’s official — Whoopi Goldberg can’t contain herself on air. The View co-host let one rip (again) during a taping of the talk show on Wednesday, Dec. 10. And this time, Ashanti was victim to the explosion.

In the middle of Ashanti explaining why she won’t be getting a flu shot this year, Goldberg passed gas and sent the singer and fellow hosts Rosie O’Donnell, Rosie Perez, and Nicolle Wallace into hysterics.

“Excuse me!” Goldberg said after fleeing her seat, while Ashanti exclaimed, “That’s the flu shot!”
