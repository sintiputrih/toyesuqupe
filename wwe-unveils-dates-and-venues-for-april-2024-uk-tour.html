WWE Unveils Dates and Venues for April 2024 UK Tour

Get ready, UK WWE fans, because the wrestling extravaganza is heading your way once again! After the epic showdown at WrestleMania 40 in April, WWE is back with some exciting news:

WWE Live Storms Back to the UK in April 2024!

WWE Live is making a triumphant return to the United Kingdom, and it’s going to be bigger and better than ever. Mark your calendars because you won’t want to miss this.

Here’s the inside scoop on the dates and locations:

  • Birmingham, UK: Brace yourselves for action on Wednesday, April 17, 2024.
  • Cardiff, Wales: Get ready for the rumble on Thursday, April 18, 2024.
  • London, UK: The capital city will be shaking on Friday, April 19, 2024.
  • Belfast, Northern Ireland: The showdown hits Belfast on Saturday, April 20, 2024.

And the best part? Tickets are about to drop like a body slam! Keep your eyes peeled because they’ll be available for purchase real soon.

Prepare yourselves, WWE fans, because you’re in for a treat. WWE Live is bringing your favorite WWE Superstars to the Resorts World Arena in Birmingham, Cardiff International Arena in Cardiff, The O2 in London, and SSE Arena in Belfast. Trust us; you don’t want to miss this epic showdown.

Stay locked on WWE.com for all the juicy details on tickets as they become available. It’s time to gear up for a wrestling extravaganza like no other!
