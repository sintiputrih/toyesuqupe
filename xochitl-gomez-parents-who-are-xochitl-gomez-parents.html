Xochitl Gomez’s parents: Who are Xochitl Gomez’s parents?

Gomez was born on April 29, 2006, in Los Angeles, California. Her mother is a set decorator for films, while her father is a construction worker. Gomez is of Mexican descent, as her father is from Mexico. She was raised in Hollywood, Los Angeles, and moved to Echo Park at age five.

The name Xochitl is pronounced as soh-cheel and means flower in the indigenous Mexican language of Nahuatl.
